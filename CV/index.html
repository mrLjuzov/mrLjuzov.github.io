За мене

Hello, I'm Jane Ljuzov, a Computer Science graduate with a Master's degree from International Slavic University. During my academic journey, I completed an internship at Joyson Safety Systems, where I gained invaluable IT experience. Currently, I work as an IT Technician at Sport Life, where we handle the repair, replacement and maintenance of the equipment in the gambling industry subsidiaries across Macedonia.

My aspiration is to contribute to a software, app or web development company, upgrading my skills and gaining further experience. I'm known for my quick learning ability and meticulous attention to detail, I'm often regarded as a perfectionist, ensuring high-quality results in all endeavors. I'm enthusiastic about continuing to expand my experience and make meaningful contributions in the tech industry.
